The New Menu of OMG Cafe is Uber Delightful!

Share on Facebook Share on Twitter Share on Google +1

Cuisines: Fast Food, Beverages

Filters: Outdoor seating, Dine-in

Attributes: Cafes, Classy

If you are tired from all the shopping at Lokhandwala and those hunger pangs are back then OMG! Oh My Gorge is just the place for you. An offbeat cafe situated near Lokhandwala Circle, it's become a place to overindulge on good food with good vibes! Known for lip smacking nonvegetarian food and their burgers they have added a new menu which includes toothsome burgers, appetizers, and shakes you should add it on your list of places to visit. 
 
When you step inside you feel the pleasing aura of the place. Make sure to notice the amusing and witty posters on the wall. They also have a classic collection of board games including Ludo, Tic Tac Toe, and Jenga for you and your squad to play till you wait for your food to be served. #LetTheGamesBegin
 
It was hard to pick and choose everything looked and sounded delicious, here's what I tried and definitely recommend:
 
1) Cottage Cheese Fingers: Crumbed cottage cheese served with spicy mango dip. The mango dip has a tart, lemon-like taste. The crunchy fingers are fried to perfection which would surely leave all paneer lovers in a state of content. 
 
2) Chermoula Chicken: Pieces of grilled chicken breast marinated with Chermoula, a traditional Moroccan spice to add spicyflavour served with their house salad. The chicken is skewered which gives the dish a juicy bland taste. 
 
3) Crispy Paneer Burger: Cottage cheese steak dipped in a batter, deep fried and served with a creamy Cilantro Mayo spread, along with French Fries on the side. If you are famished, this burger alone is equivalent to a multi-course meal. It will be irresistible to lick your fingers after finishing the dish. If you are looking for nonvegetarian options for burgers then the Fita & Lamb Burger and Fish Fillet Burger must be tried.
 
4) Pesto Paneer Wafflizza: If you love waffles and pizza both, you’re in for a treat. The Wafflizza is a pizza with the base of a waffle. The dish contains pillowy chunks of cottage cheese & bell pepper tossed in herby pesto sauce and topped with mozzarella cheese and who isn't the fan of cheese! It's a melt-in-your-mouth kind of dish. 
 
 
5) Chocolate Overload Milkshake: This freak sake has layers of chocolate, which include brownie, ice cream, chocolate bits, topped with chocolate sauce and whipped cream and at the first sip you know you are not going to share this shake. If you are not a chocolate person (why?) you can also try their newly added Red Velvet Milkshake or Peanut Butter Thin Shake.
  
If you gasped or went “OMG!” reading about it do check out OMG Resto Café at Lokhandwala on your next outing.
